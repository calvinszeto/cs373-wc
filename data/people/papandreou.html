Summary

As Prime Minister of Greece from October 6th, 2009 to November 11th, 2011, George Papandreou served during Greece's sovereign debt crisis. As Prime Minister, Papandreou requested financial aid via a Stand-By Agreement from the IMF. However, the demands of austerity from the IMF caused mass protests and riots in Greece, some of which turned violent.
