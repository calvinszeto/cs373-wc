People

First Lady Michelle LaVaughn Robinson Obama

Michelle LaVaughn Robinson Obama is the first African-American First Lady of the United States and wife to the 44th President of the United States, Barack Obama.

Doctor Jonas Edward Salk

Jonas Salk was an American medical researcher and virologist know for his discovery of the first safe polio vaccine.

Kenneth Feinberg

An American attorney with his own firm, Feinberg Rozen, Kenneth Feinberg was appointed the administrator of the BP Deepwater Horizon Disaster Victim Compensation Fund. Feinberg was subject to much controversy regarding the fund, including a conflict of interest accusation due to BP paying Feinberg to administer the fund. Additionally, the Center for Justice and Democracy sent a letter to BP raising issues about the lack of transparency regarding the fund.

Former Prime Minister of Greece George Papandreou

As Prime Minister of Greece from October 6th, 2009 to November 11th, 2011, George Papandreou served during Greece's sovereign debt crisis. As Prime Minister, Papandreou requested financial aid via a Stand-By Agreement from the IMF. However, the demands of austerity from the IMF caused mass protests and riots in Greece, some of which turned violent.
