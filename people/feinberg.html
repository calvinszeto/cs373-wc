Summary

An American attorney with his own firm, Feinberg Rozen, Kenneth Feinberg was appointed the administrator of the BP Deepwater Horizon Disaster Victim Compensation Fund. Feinberg was subject to much controversy regarding the fund, including a conflict of interest accusation due to BP paying Feinberg to administer the fund. Additionally, the Center for Justice and Democracy sent a letter to BP raising issues about the lack of transparency regarding the fund.
